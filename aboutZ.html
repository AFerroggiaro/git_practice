Treatment Effectiveness

Zhealus is an independent, non-affiliated, quality improvement organization. We focus directly on the patient = you or your family member. Our concept is to give you more tools and power in the healthcare relationship so that your diagnosis-treatment-recovery is successful and maximized.

Our value proposition: We measure, analyze, and organize personalized patient recovery input providing timely, efficient, actionable feedback to support treatment effectiveness and long term practice evolution. Therefore, as independent agents, we inform patients, doctors and providers, hospitals and health systems, insurers on the right way to treat illness and injury.
